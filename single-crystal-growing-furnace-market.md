<p><p>Single crystal growing furnace is a technology used to produce high-quality single crystals for various applications. The market includes different types of furnaces, such as the Czochralski Process (Cz) furnace, which uses crucibles and pulling mechanisms to grow crystals with high purity and large diameters. The Float Zone (FZ) furnace employs induction heating to melt the precursor material and facilitate crystal growth. The Bridgman furnace utilizes a temperature gradient to solidify molten material into a single crystal. Other types of furnaces may include techniques like the Horizontal Bridgman and the Traveling Solvent Floating Zone, each with unique characteristics and applications.</p></p>
