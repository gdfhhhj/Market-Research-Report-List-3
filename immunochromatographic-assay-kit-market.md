<p><p>The Immunochromatographic Assay Kit Market includes various types of assays. The Sandwich Assay is a method where the analyte is sandwiched between two antibodies, enabling its detection. Competitive Assay involves the competition of the analyte and a labeled analogue for binding to the antibody. Multiplex Detection Assay allows the simultaneous detection of multiple analytes in a single sample. These assays play a crucial role in diagnostic applications, enabling accurate and efficient detection of various diseases and medical conditions.</p></p>
