<p><p>The paediatric radiology market includes various types of imaging techniques to diagnose diseases in children. Ultrasound uses sound waves to create images of internal organs. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) uses strong magnets and radio waves to produce detailed images of the body. Computed Tomography (CT) creates cross-sectional images using X-rays. X-ray imaging uses low-dose radiation to visualize bones and some soft tissues. Furthermore, there are other imaging techniques like nuclear medicine, positron emission tomography (PET), and fluoroscopy used in paediatric radiology to aid in accurate diagnosis.</p></p>
