<p><p>Wood flame retardant market can be divided into two main types - inorganic type and organic type. Inorganic flame retardants are made from minerals or salts and work by physically interrupting the combustion process. Organic flame retardants contain carbon and nitrogen molecules and work by releasing gases that inhibit the spread of flames. Both types have their own advantages and disadvantages, depending on factors such as cost, effectiveness, and environmental impact. The choice between the two types depends on the specific needs of the application.</p></p>
