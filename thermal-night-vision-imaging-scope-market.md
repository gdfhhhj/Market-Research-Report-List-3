<p><strong>The Thermal Night Vision Imaging Scope Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Un-Cooled Thermal Imaging</li><li>Cryogenically Cooled Thermal Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal night vision imaging scopes come in two main types: un-cooled thermal imaging and cryogenically cooled thermal imaging. Un-cooled thermal imaging scopes are more affordable and compact, making them popular for consumer use. They use microbolometer technology to detect infrared radiation. Cryogenically cooled thermal imaging scopes offer higher sensitivity and resolution, but are more expensive and require cooling systems to operate. They are typically used in military and professional applications where utmost precision and clarity are essential.</p></p>
