<p><p>Medical Hand Orthosis (HO) Market comprises of two main types: Dynamic Orthotics and Static Orthotics. </p><p>Dynamic Orthotics are designed to provide continuous passive motion for improved functional outcomes, muscle activation, and joint mobilization. These orthotics are commonly used in conditions like stroke, traumatic brain injury, and spinal cord injury.</p><p>On the other hand, Static Orthotics are designed to provide immobilization and support to help in the healing process of fractures, sprains, and other injuries that require stable positioning of the hand.</p></p>
