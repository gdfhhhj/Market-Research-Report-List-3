<p><p>Medical oral nutrition supplements market consists of three main types: liquid supplements, semi-solid supplements, and powder supplements. Semi-solid supplements are pre-thickened and are often used for patients with dysphagia. Powder supplements are versatile and can be mixed with various foods or beverages for customized nutrition support.
