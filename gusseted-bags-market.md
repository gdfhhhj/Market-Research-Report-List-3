<p><p>Gusseted bags are a popular packaging solution offered in two main types: side gusseted bags and bottom gusseted bags. Side gusseted bags have expandable sides that allow the bag to hold more volume, making them ideal for packaging bulkier items. Bottom gusseted bags have a gusset at the bottom of the bag, providing stability and allowing the bag to stand upright on its own. Both types of gusseted bags are commonly used in the food industry for packaging items like coffee, snacks, and pet food.</p></p>
