<p><p>Connector Headers Market Types can be categorized into Double Connector Headers and Single Connector Headers. Double Connector Headers consist of two rows of pins and are ideal for applications requiring a higher number of connections. Single Connector Headers, on the other hand, have only one row of pins and are suitable for simpler applications with fewer connections. Both types offer a reliable way to connect electronic components, but Double Connector Headers are more versatile in terms of accommodating a larger number of connections.</p></p>
