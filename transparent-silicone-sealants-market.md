<p><p>Transparent silicone sealants market is divided into two types: single component and double component. Single component sealants come ready to use and do not require any mixing before application. They are convenient and easy to use for quick sealing projects. On the other hand, double component sealants come in two separate parts that need to be mixed together before application. This type is more commonly used for larger projects that require a stronger bond and longer curing times. Both types offer effective sealing and waterproofing properties.</p></p>
