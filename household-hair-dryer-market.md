<p><strong>The Household Hair Dryer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Hair Dryer</li><li>Axial Hair Dryer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Household hair dryers can be classified into two main market types based on their internal mechanism: centrifugal hair dryers and axial hair dryers. </p><p>Centrifugal hair dryers use a rotating fan to create airflow, which then heats up and dries the hair. This type of hair dryer is known for its quick drying time and powerful airflow.</p><p>Axial hair dryers, on the other hand, use a propeller-like fan to create airflow, resulting in a more gentle and consistent drying experience. This type of hair dryer is preferred by those looking for a quieter and more precise drying process.</p></p>
