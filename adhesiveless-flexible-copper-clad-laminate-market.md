<p><p>Adhesiveless Flexible Copper Clad Laminate is mainly divided into two types: Single-sided Non-adhesive FCCL and Double-sided Non-adhesive FCCL. The Single-sided Non-adhesive FCCL market involves a flexible copper clad laminate that has copper foil on only one side and no adhesive layer, making it suitable for applications where flexibility and low weight are essential. On the other hand, the Double-sided Non-adhesive FCCL market includes a flexible copper clad laminate with copper foil on both sides, providing increased flexibility and versatility for more complex electronic applications.</p></p>
