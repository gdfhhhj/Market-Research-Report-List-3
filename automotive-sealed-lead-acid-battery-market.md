<p><p>The Automotive Sealed Lead-acid Battery market includes two main types of batteries: AGM batteries and Gel batteries. AGM (Absorbent Glass Mat) batteries use a fiberglass mat to absorb the electrolyte, providing a spill-proof and maintenance-free option. Gel batteries use a silica gel to immobilize the electrolyte, making them more resistant to vibration and better suited for deep cycle applications. Both types offer reliable performance, long cycle life, and are commonly used in automotive and industrial applications.</p></p>
