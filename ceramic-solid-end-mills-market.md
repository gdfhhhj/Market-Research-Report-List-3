<p><p>Ceramic solid end mills are classified into three main types based on the material of the cutting edge: iron-based, nickel-based, and cobalt-based. Iron-based ceramic end mills are known for their high strength and wear resistance, making them suitable for cutting tough materials. Nickel-based end mills offer excellent heat resistance and are commonly used in high-speed cutting applications. Cobalt-based ceramic end mills are preferred for their high toughness and versatility in various cutting tasks. Each type has its own unique properties and advantages in the market.</p></p>
