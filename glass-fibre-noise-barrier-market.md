<p><p>Glass Fibre Noise Barrier market is segmented into two types: Ground-Mounted Noise Barrier and Structure-Mounted Noise Barrier. Ground-Mounted Noise Barriers are installed on the ground between the noise source and the receiver, typically along highways or railways. Structure-Mounted Noise Barriers are installed on existing structures, such as bridges or buildings, to deflect or absorb noise. Both types of barriers are made of glass fibre, offering durability and sound absorption properties to reduce noise pollution effectively.</p></p>
