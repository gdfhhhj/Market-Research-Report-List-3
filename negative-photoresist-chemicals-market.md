Thinner: Used to dilute the photoresist solution to achieve the desired consistency for application.</p><p>2. Flushing Agent: Employed to clean and remove excess photoresist from surfaces after the patterning process.</p><p>3. Stripper: Utilized to completely remove the hardened and developed photoresist from substrates.</p><p>4. Other: Includes additives, surfactants, and adhesion promoters that enhance the performance and properties of negative photoresist chemicals in different applications.</p></p>
