<p><p>Solid state contactors come in various types based on their mounting options. PCB mounting contactors are designed to be directly soldered onto printed circuit boards for integration into electronic systems. DIN rail mounting contactors are designed to be easily mounted onto standard DIN rail systems for convenient installation in control panels. Panel-mount contactors are designed to be mounted directly onto control panels or enclosures. Surface-mount contactors are mounted directly onto a surface with screws or adhesive. Other market types include specialty contactors for specific applications.</p></p>
