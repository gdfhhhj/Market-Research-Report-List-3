<p><p>Gold mining can be categorized into different types based on the methods used. Placer mining involves using water to separate gold from sediment. Sluicing uses a channel of flowing water to trap gold particles. Gold panning involves using a pan to separate gold from sediment. Dredging involves using a large machine to suck up sediment and extract gold. Hard-rock mining involves drilling into solid rock to extract gold. Rocker box mining uses a box-like contraption to separate gold from sediment.</p></p>
