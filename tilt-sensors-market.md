<p><strong>The Tilt Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid Pendulum</li><li>Liquid Pendulum</li><li>Gas Pendulum</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tilt sensors are categorized into three main types based on the material used for the pendulum inside the sensor: solid pendulum, liquid pendulum, and gas pendulum. Solid pendulum tilt sensors use a solid material, such as metal, for the pendulum. Liquid pendulum sensors use a liquid, often mercury or oil, for the pendulum. Gas pendulum sensors use a gas, typically nitrogen or helium. Each type of tilt sensor has its own advantages and applications, making them suitable for a variety of industries and environments.</p></p>
