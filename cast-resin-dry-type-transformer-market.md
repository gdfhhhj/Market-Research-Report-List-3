<p><strong>The Cast Resin Dry Type Transformer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Concentric Type</li><li>Overlap Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cast resin dry type transformers are available in two main market types: concentric type and overlap type. </p><p>Concentric type transformers have their windings arranged in a concentric manner, where the high voltage winding is placed at the center and the low voltage winding surrounds it. </p><p>Overlap type transformers have their windings overlapped in a compact manner, allowing for a more efficient use of space. Both types offer efficient and reliable performance in various applications, meeting the needs of different industries and sectors.</p></p>
