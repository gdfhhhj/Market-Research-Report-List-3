<p><p>Automotive glass for sidelites comes in three main types: tempered, laminated, and other. Tempered glass is heat-treated for increased strength and safety, shattering into small, blunt pieces upon impact. Laminated glass consists of two layers of glass with a layer of polyvinyl butyral in between for added durability and resistance to breaking. The "other" category may include specialty glass types or combinations of tempered and laminated glass. Each type offers unique benefits in terms of safety, strength, and customization options for automotive sidelites.</p></p>
