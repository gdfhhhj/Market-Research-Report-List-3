<p><p>Coated textiles can be categorized into different types based on the material used for coating. Polymer-coated textiles are those which have a layer of synthetic polymers applied to the fabric for improved durability and performance. Rubber-coated textiles, on the other hand, are fabrics coated with rubber to enhance water resistance and flexibility. Other types of coatings may include PVC, TPU, or silicone coatings, each offering specific benefits such as increased abrasion resistance or UV protection. These diverse coatings cater to various industries and applications.</p></p>
