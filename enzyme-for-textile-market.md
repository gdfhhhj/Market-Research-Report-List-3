<p><p>Enzymes for textile market types include cellulase, amylase, catalase, pectinase, and others. Cellulase enzymes are used to break down cellulose fibers in fabrics, while amylase enzymes are used for starch removal. Catalase enzymes help in the removal of hydrogen peroxide from fabrics, and pectinase enzymes aid in the removal of pectin residues from textiles. Other types of enzymes may have various functions in the textile industry, such as improving dyeing processes or enhancing fabric softness.</p></p>
