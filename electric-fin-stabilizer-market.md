<p><p>Electric fin stabilizers are divided into two main types: retractable and non-retractable. Retractable stabilizers can be folded up when not in use, minimizing drag and improving fuel efficiency. Non-retractable stabilizers, on the other hand, are fixed in place and operate continuously while the vessel is in motion. Both types provide stability and reduce rolling motion, enhancing comfort for passengers and crew onboard. The choice between retractable and non-retractable stabilizers depends on the specific requirements and preferences of the vessel owner.</p></p>
