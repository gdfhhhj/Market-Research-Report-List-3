<p><p>The residential electric fryers market consists of two main types: deep fat fryers and air fryers. Deep fat fryers are traditional fryers that use a large amount of oil to fry food, resulting in a crispy texture but also adding more fat to the food. On the other hand, air fryers utilize hot air circulation to cook food, requiring little to no oil and producing healthier, less greasy results. Both types offer convenient and quick cooking options for frying various types of food at home.</p></p>
