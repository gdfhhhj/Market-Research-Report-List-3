The growing awareness regarding hand hygiene and the increasing prevalence of infectious diseases are major drivers for market growth. Additionally, the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic has further accelerated the demand for hand sanitizers worldwide.</p><p>There has been a shift in consumer preferences towards natural and organic hand sanitizers, driven by concerns over harsh chemicals and skin irritation. Many manufacturers are now offering plant-based or alcohol-free hand sanitizers to cater to this demand. Moreover, the market has witnessed a surge in the production of sanitizers with added moisturizing agents to counter the drying effects of alcohol.</p><p>The rise in online retailing has also contributed to market growth, as consumers increasingly prefer the convenience of purchasing hand sanitizers online.
