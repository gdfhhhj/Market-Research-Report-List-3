<p><p>Fresh agricultural products packaging market offers various types of packaging solutions to extend the shelf life of perishable products. Modified Atmosphere Packaging (MAP) involves altering the atmosphere inside the package to slow down deterioration. Vacuum Skin Packaging (VSP) involves tightly wrapping products in a film, creating a skin-tight seal. Vacuum Thermoformed Packaging (VTP) uses vacuum technology to remove air from the package, reducing oxygen levels. Other packaging types include biodegradable packaging, moisture-resistant packaging, and active packaging.</p></p>
