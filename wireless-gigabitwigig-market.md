<p><p>Wireless GigaBit (WiGig) technology refers to high-speed wireless communication that operates in the gigabit range. There are two market types related to WiGig: 802.11ac and 802.11ad. The 802.11ac market focuses on providing high-speed wireless connections in the 5 GHz frequency band. It is commonly used for Wi-Fi connectivity in homes, businesses, and public spaces. On the other hand, the 802.11ad market operates in the 60 GHz frequency band, offering even faster data rates over shorter distances. This market type is more suitable for specialized applications like wireless docking stations, virtual reality devices, and wireless display connections.</p></p>
