<p><ul><li>Pressure Die Casting</li><li>Vacuum Die Casting</li><li>Squeeze Die Casting</li><li>Semi-Solid Die Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive part die casting encompasses various types, including pressure die casting, vacuum die casting, squeeze die casting, and semi-solid die casting. Pressure die casting involves injecting molten metal into a mold under high pressure. Vacuum die casting utilizes a vacuum to remove air from the mold before pouring molten metal. Squeeze die casting involves applying high pressure to the molten metal to ensure it fills the mold completely. Semi-solid die casting uses partially solidified metal for a more precise and uniform final product.</p></p>
