<p><p>Courier tape market can be categorized into two main types based on the adhesive used: Single-Sided Adhesive Tapes and Double-Sided Adhesive Tapes. Single-sided adhesive tapes are coated with adhesive on one side only, making them suitable for sealing packages securely. On the other hand, double-sided adhesive tapes have adhesive on both sides, allowing them to bond two surfaces together. Both types of courier tapes are essential for packaging and shipping applications, providing different levels of adhesion and strength depending on the specific needs of users.</p></p>
