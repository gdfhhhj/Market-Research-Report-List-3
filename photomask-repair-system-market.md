<p><p>Photomask Repair System market includes three main types of technologies; Laser Technology, Focused Ion Beam (FIB) Technology, and Nanomachining Technology. Laser Technology utilizes high-powered lasers to ablate or remove defects on the photomask. FIB Technology uses a focused beam of ions to precisely remove defects or add materials to the photomask. Nanomachining Technology involves using nanoscale cutting tools to repair defects on the photomask. Each of these technologies offers unique advantages and applications in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
