<p><strong>The Professional Helicopter Simulator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FFS</li><li>ATD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Professional Helicopter Simulator market consists of two major types: Full Flight Simulators (FFS) and Advanced Training Devices (ATD). FFS are high-end simulators that replicate a helicopter's flight characteristics and systems with high fidelity. They provide a realistic training experience for pilots, including motion, visual, and sound cues. On the other hand, ATDs are lower-cost simulators that focus on specific flight tasks and procedural training. While they lack certain features of FFS, they still offer a valuable training tool for pilots to improve their skills and knowledge.</p></p>
