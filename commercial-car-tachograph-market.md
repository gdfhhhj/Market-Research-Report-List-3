<p><p>The commercial car tachograph market can be classified into two main types: integrated and portable. They are typically used by larger fleet companies that need continuous monitoring and recording of their vehicles' data. Portable tachographs, on the other hand, are detachable devices that can be easily transferred between vehicles. They are commonly used by smaller businesses or individual drivers who require more flexibility in their tachograph usage.</p></p>
