<p><p>Relays for 5G are crucial components in telecommunications infrastructure, providing efficient switching and control functions. There are two main types in the market: Electromagnetic Relays and Solid State Relays (SSRs). Electromagnetic relays use electromagnetic principles to open or close circuits, while SSRs use semiconductors for switching. Both types play a significant role in ensuring reliable signal transmission and data transfer in 5G networks. With the growing demand for high-speed and low-latency communication, these relay types continue to evolve to meet the requirements of the 5G market.</p></p>
