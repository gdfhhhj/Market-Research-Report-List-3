<p><strong>The Test Handler Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity-Feed Handlers</li><li>Turret Handlers</li><li>Pick-and-Place Handlers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Test handler systems come in various types to accommodate different testing needs. Gravity-feed handlers utilize the force of gravity to move devices through the testing process efficiently. Turret handlers use a rotating turret to access multiple testing stations without the need for additional handling. Pick-and-place handlers use robotic arms to precisely place devices for testing. Each type has its own advantages and is suitable for different testing environments, making it important for companies to choose the right handler system for their specific needs.</p></p>
