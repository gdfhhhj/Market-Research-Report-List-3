<p><p>Metal cases for SMT packaging are commonly classified into two main types: TO cases and flat cases. TO cases are cylindrical in shape with a flat bottom and are commonly used for housing components such as transistors and thyristors. Flat cases, on the other hand, have a rectangular or square shape with a flat surface and are often used for integrated circuit packages. Both types of metal cases provide protection for electronic components and help to ensure the integrity of the circuitry during manufacturing and operation.</p></p>
