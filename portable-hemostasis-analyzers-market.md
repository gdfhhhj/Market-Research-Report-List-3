<p><p>Portable hemostasis analyzers are medical devices used to measure the clotting ability of blood. The market types of these analyzers include optical detection and mechanical detection. Optical detection systems use light to measure the clot formation and assess hemostasis. They are highly accurate and provide fast results. On the other hand, mechanical detection systems rely on mechanical principles to detect clot formation, offering reliable and reproducible results. Both types of analyzers are designed to be portable, allowing for convenient and efficient use in various healthcare settings.</p></p>
