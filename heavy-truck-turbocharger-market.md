<p><p>The heavy truck turbocharger market can be categorized into two main types - Mono Turbo and Twin Turbo. Mono Turbo systems have one turbocharger while Twin Turbo systems have two turbochargers working in tandem. Mono Turbo systems are more commonly found in heavy trucks due to their simpler design and lower cost, while Twin Turbo systems offer improved performance and efficiency by utilizing two smaller turbochargers operating at different speeds to deliver more power and reduce turbo lag.</p></p>
