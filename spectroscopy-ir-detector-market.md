<p><p>Spectroscopy IR detector market is divided into three types based on the wavelength range: short wave, medium, and long wave. Short wave detectors operate within the 1-3 micron range and are commonly used for molecular analysis in industries like pharmaceuticals and chemicals. Medium wave detectors work in the 3-8 micron range and are utilized for applications such as gas analysis and environmental monitoring. Long wave detectors cover the 8-15 micron range and are mainly used in industries like food processing and thermal imaging for security and surveillance purposes.</p></p>
