<p><p>PEHD Tube Market is categorized into three types based on their material composition: PE80, PE100, and Others. PE80 tubes are made from high-density polyethylene with an 80 MPa minimum design stress, while PE100 tubes have a higher minimum design stress of 100 MPa. The Others category includes tubes made from different variations of high-density polyethylene that do not fit into the PE80 or PE100 classification. Each type of tube has specific characteristics and applications based on their material composition and design stress levels.</p></p>
