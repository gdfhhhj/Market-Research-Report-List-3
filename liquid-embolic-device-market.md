<p><p>The liquid embolic device market includes liquid embolic vials and liquid embolic prefilled injectables. Liquid embolic vials are containers that hold embolic materials, used for the treatment of vascular abnormalities. Meanwhile, liquid embolic prefilled injectables are pre-filled syringes or catheters containing embolic agents, ready to be directly injected into the affected blood vessels. Both types of devices are utilized in minimally invasive procedures to block blood flow to abnormal vessels, such as arteriovenous malformations or aneurysms, helping to prevent complications and promote patient recovery.</p></p>
