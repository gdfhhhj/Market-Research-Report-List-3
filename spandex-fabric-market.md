<p><strong>The Spandex Fabric Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solution Dry Spinning</li><li>Solution Wet Spinning</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Spandex Fabric market can be divided into two main types: Solution Dry Spinning and Solution Wet Spinning. In Solution Dry Spinning, the spandex polymer solution is extruded through a spinneret into a chamber of heated air, causing the solvent to evaporate and leaving behind solid spandex fibers. In Solution Wet Spinning, the spandex polymer solution is extruded into a liquid bath, where the solvent is rapidly precipitated out, forming solid spandex fibers. Each method has its own advantages and applications in the textile industry.</p></p>
