<p><p>In gravure printing, there are three main types of inks used: water-based inks, solvent-based inks, and UV-cured inks. Water-based inks are environmentally friendly and contain high water content, making them easy to clean up. Solvent-based inks use volatile organic compounds (VOCs) as a drying agent, making them quick-drying and durable. UV-cured inks are cured instantly with UV light, making them highly resistant to abrasion and chemicals. Each type of ink offers different benefits and is suited to specific printing needs.</p></p>
