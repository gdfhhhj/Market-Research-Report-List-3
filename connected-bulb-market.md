<p><p>The Connected Bulb market consists of three main types: ZigBee, Wi-Fi, and Bluetooth. ZigBee connected bulbs utilize a wireless communication protocol for low-power, low-cost networks. Wi-Fi connected bulbs operate over standard Wi-Fi networks, allowing for easy setup and integration with other smart devices. Bluetooth connected bulbs connect directly to a user's smartphone or tablet, providing quick and convenient control. Each type offers unique advantages and features, catering to different preferences and needs in the smart lighting industry.</p></p>
