<p><p>Aluminum foil shielding tape comes in two main types - one-sided and double-sided. One-sided tape has a layer of conductive adhesive on only one side of the foil, making it suitable for applications where only one side needs to be shielded. Double-sided tape, on the other hand, has conductive adhesive on both sides of the foil, providing increased shielding capabilities for applications that require protection on both sides. Both types of tape are commonly used in electronics, construction, and automotive industries.</p></p>
