<p><p>Automatic endoscope cleaners are devices used for cleaning and disinfecting endoscopes after use. They are classified into two types: single chamber cleaners and multi chamber cleaners. Single chamber cleaners have only one cleaning and disinfection chamber where the endoscope is placed for the cleaning process. On the other hand, multi chamber cleaners have multiple chambers for different stages of the cleaning process, including cleaning, rinsing, and disinfection. Both types of cleaners are designed to ensure effective and safe cleaning of endoscopes, ultimately reducing the risk of infections during medical procedures.</p></p>
