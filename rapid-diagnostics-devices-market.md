<p><ul><li>Over-The-Counter (OTC) Rapid Diagnostics Test</li><li>Professional Rapid Diagnostics Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rapid diagnostics devices market can be classified into two main types - Over-The-Counter (OTC) rapid diagnostics test and professional rapid diagnostics test. OTC rapid diagnostics tests are designed for self-administration by individuals at home or non-clinical settings. These tests are convenient, user-friendly, and provide quick results for diseases like pregnancy, HIV, flu, etc. On the other hand, professional rapid diagnostics tests are performed by healthcare professionals in clinical settings. These tests are more complex and accurate, enabling clinicians to diagnose and monitor various diseases promptly for effective treatment.</p></p>
