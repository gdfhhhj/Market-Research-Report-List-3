<p><p>Transient generators are devices used to simulate transient conditions in electrical systems for testing purposes. Surge generators produce high voltage surges to determine the immunity of equipment to transient voltage spikes. Burst generators produce short duration pulses to assess the susceptibility of devices to bursts of interference. Both types of generators are essential for ensuring the reliability and safety of electrical and electronic equipment in various industries such as telecommunications, automotive, and aerospace. These generators are crucial for testing equipment to meet industry standards and regulations.</p></p>
