<p><p>The Cold Laser Therapy Device Market includes two types of lasers: Continuous Laser and Pulse Laser. Continuous Laser emits a continuous beam of low-level laser light, providing a steady dose of energy to the treatment area. It is typically used for chronic conditions and tissue repair. Pulse Laser, on the other hand, emits laser light in short bursts, allowing for deeper penetration and better targeting of specific areas. This type of laser is commonly used for acute injuries and pain relief. Both types offer non-invasive and safe treatments for various medical conditions.</p></p>
