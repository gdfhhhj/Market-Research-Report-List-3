<p><p>Auto Electromagnetic Compatibility Test Market includes various types such as Bulk Current Injection (BCI), Conducted Emissions (CE), Conducted Transient Emissions (CTE), Conducted Transient Immunity (CTI), Electrostatic Discharge (ESD), Radiated Emissions (RE), and Radiated Immunity (RI). BCI tests involve injecting current directly into the product to assess its ability to handle electromagnetic interference. CE tests measure the emissions conducted through power and signal lines. CTE and CTI tests evaluate the transient emissions and immunity of a product. ESD tests assess the product's ability to withstand electrostatic discharges. RE and RI tests analyze the product's emissions and immunity to radiated interference.</p></p>
