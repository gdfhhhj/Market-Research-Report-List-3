<p><p>Cylindrical magnetic sensors are available in two main types: micro magnetic sensors and standard magnetic sensors. Micro magnetic sensors are smaller in size and provide higher sensitivity and resolution compared to standard magnetic sensors. They are commonly used in applications where space is limited. Standard magnetic sensors, on the other hand, are larger in size and offer lower sensitivity and resolution but are more cost-effective and suitable for less demanding applications. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in detecting and measuring magnetic fields in various industries.</p></p>
