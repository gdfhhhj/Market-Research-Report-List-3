<p><ul><li>High G</li><li>Low G</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial alginate market can be categorized into High G and Low G types based on the gel strength. High G alginate has a strong gel strength, making it ideal for applications requiring quick setting and high firmness. On the other hand, Low G alginate has a lower gel strength, providing more flexibility and slower setting time. Each type caters to different industrial needs and preferences, allowing manufacturers to choose the most suitable alginate for their specific applications.</p></p>
