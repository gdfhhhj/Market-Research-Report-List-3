<p><strong>The 3D Solder Paste Inspection SPI System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Off-line SPI System</li><li>In-line SPI System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Off-line SPI systems are used for post-print inspection of solder paste. They are typically separate machines that inspect solder paste quality after the printing process. In-line SPI systems, on the other hand, are integrated into the production line and inspect solder paste quality in real-time during the printing process. They provide immediate feedback to operators, allowing for adjustments to be made quickly to ensure optimal solder paste quality. Both types of systems are important tools for ensuring quality and reliability in PCB manufacturing.</p></p>
