<p><p>The V2X market for SUVs refers to the adoption and implementation of Vehicle-to-Everything (V2X) communication technology in the SUV market. V2X includes three types of communication: Vehicle-to-Vehicle (V2V), Vehicle-to-Infrastructure (V2I), and Vehicle-to-Pedestrian (V2P). V2V enables vehicles to exchange real-time information to improve safety and avoid accidents. V2I connects vehicles to infrastructure like traffic lights and road signs, allowing for improved traffic management and efficient driving. V2P facilitates communication between vehicles and pedestrians, enhancing pedestrian safety and reducing accidents.</p></p>
