Absorbance Plate Readers measure the amount of light absorbed by the sample, which can provide information about the concentration of molecules in the sample. Luminescence Plate Readers are used to measure the emission of light from samples, which can provide information about biological processes such as enzyme activity or gene expression.</p></p>
