<p><p>The AC-DC power conversion market can be divided into two main types: external AC-DC power and embedded AC-DC power. External AC-DC power refers to power supplies that are separate from the device they are powering, such as laptop chargers or power adapters. Embedded AC-DC power, on the other hand, refers to power supplies that are integrated directly into the device, such as in televisions or computers. Both markets play a crucial role in providing efficient and reliable power conversion solutions for various electronic devices.</p></p>
