<p><p>There are two main types of Automotive USB Power Delivery Systems - USB Type-A and USB Type-C markets. USB Type-A is a traditional, rectangular connector found in many older vehicles. It is mainly used for basic charging and data transfer functions. On the other hand, USB Type-C is a newer, smaller, reversible connector that is becoming more popular in modern vehicles. It offers faster charging speeds and can support various advanced functionalities like video output and fast data transfer.</p></p>
