<p><p>PCTA, or Polyethylene Terephthalate Copolymer, is available in three main market types: Injection Grade, Extruded Grade, and Blow Molding Grade. Injection Grade PCTA is used for producing precision parts through injection molding processes. Extruded Grade PCTA is commonly used for extrusion processes to create films, fibers, or sheets. Blow Molding Grade PCTA is suitable for blow molding applications where hollow products like bottles or containers are manufactured. Each grade is tailored to specific manufacturing processes and end-use applications.</p></p>
