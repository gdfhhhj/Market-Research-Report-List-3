<p><p>Automotive grippers can be categorized into electric grippers and pneumatic grippers. Electric grippers are powered by electrical motors, which allow for precise control and positioning. They are ideal for applications that require high accuracy and repeatability. On the other hand, pneumatic grippers are powered by compressed air, making them more cost-effective and suitable for high-speed applications. Both types of grippers play a crucial role in the automotive industry, providing efficient handling and manipulation of various components during manufacturing processes.</p></p>
