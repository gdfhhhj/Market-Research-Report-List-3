<p><p>Medical occluder devices are used to repair various heart defects such as atrial septal defect (ASD), ventricular septal defect (VSD), patent ductus arteriosus (PDA), patent foramen ovale (PFO), and left atrial appendage (LAA) closure. ASD occluders are used to close abnormal openings between the heart's upper chambers. VSD occluders are used to close abnormal openings between the heart's lower chambers. PDA occluders are used to close the duct that connects the aorta and pulmonary artery. PFO occluders are used to close a hole between the heart's upper chambers. LAA occluders are used to prevent blood clots in patients with atrial fibrillation.</p></p>
