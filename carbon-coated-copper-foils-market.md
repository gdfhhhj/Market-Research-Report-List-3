Thin copper foil, ranging from 12-18μm, is suitable for flexible printed circuits. Common copper foil, between 18-70μm, is widely used in PCB manufacturing. Thick copper foil, with a thickness greater than 70μm, is used in high current applications. Each market type caters to specific industry needs and applications.</p></p>
