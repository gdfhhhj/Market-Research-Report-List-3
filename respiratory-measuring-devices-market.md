<p><p>Respiratory measuring devices market includes pulse oximeters, spirometers, capnographs, and peak flow meters. Pulse oximeters measure oxygen saturation in the blood, spirometers assess lung function, capnographs monitor carbon dioxide levels in exhaled breath, and peak flow meters measure how fast air can be blown out of the lungs. These devices play a crucial role in diagnosing and monitoring respiratory conditions such as asthma, chronic obstructive pulmonary disease, and sleep apnea, leading to an increasing demand for these devices in the healthcare industry.</p></p>
