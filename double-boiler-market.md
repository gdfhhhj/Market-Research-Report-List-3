<p><p>A double boiler is a kitchen tool used for gentle heating and melting of delicate ingredients such as chocolate, butter, and sauces. It consists of two pots, one smaller pot that fits inside the larger pot. The smaller pot is filled with the ingredient to be heated, while the larger pot is filled with water which is brought to a simmer. The indirect heat from the water gently heats the ingredients in the smaller pot, preventing them from scorching or burning.</p><p>The Double Boiler Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.5% during the forecast period.
