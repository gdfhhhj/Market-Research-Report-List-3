<p><strong>The Automatic Plastic Sorting Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chute-Type Sorting Machine</li><li>Belt-Type Sorting Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic plastic sorting machine market includes chute-type sorting machines and belt-type sorting machines. Chute-type sorting machines use gravity to separate different types of plastic based on their weight and shape as they slide down a chute. On the other hand, belt-type sorting machines use conveyor belts to transport plastic items past sensors that identify and sort them based on predetermined criteria. Both types of machines are widely used in recycling facilities and manufacturing plants to streamline the sorting process and improve efficiency.</p></p>
