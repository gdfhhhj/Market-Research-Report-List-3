<p><p>Multiaxial Load Frames market includes two main types - Axial / Torsional Test Systems and Planar Biaxial Test Systems. Axial / Torsional Test Systems are designed to apply both axial and torsional loads simultaneously, allowing for testing of materials under combined loading conditions. Planar Biaxial Test Systems are used to apply load in two perpendicular directions within a specific plane, enabling researchers to study the complex mechanical behavior of materials.Both types of systems are crucial for evaluating the strength and durability of materials in various industries.</p></p>
