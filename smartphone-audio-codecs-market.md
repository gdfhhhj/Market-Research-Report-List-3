<p><strong>The Smartphone Audio Codecs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lossless Compression</li><li>Lossy Compression</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smartphone audio codecs come in two main types: Lossless Compression and Lossy Compression. Lossless Compression preserves all the original audio data during encoding and decoding, resulting in higher audio quality but larger file sizes. Lossy Compression, on the other hand, reduces file sizes by discarding some audio data during compression, which may lead to a slight loss in audio quality. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, with Lossless Compression appealing to audiophiles seeking the highest quality and Lossy Compression catering to those prioritizing efficient storage.</p></p>
