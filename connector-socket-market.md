<p><p>Connector Socket market types include PLCC Socket, DIP Socket, IC Socket, LGA Socket, and Transistor Socket. PLCC Socket, or Plastic Leaded Chip Carrier Socket, is commonly used in integrated circuits. DIP Socket, or Dual In-line Package Socket, is used for connecting integrated circuits. IC Socket, or Integrated Circuit Socket, is used for connecting IC chips. LGA Socket, or Land Grid Array Socket, is used for connecting processors. Transistor Socket is used for connecting transistors in electronic circuits. Each type serves a specific purpose in the electronic components industry.</p></p>
