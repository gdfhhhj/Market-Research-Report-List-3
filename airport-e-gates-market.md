<p><p>Airport E-Gates are categorized into three main types: Fully Automatic, Semi-Automatic, and Other. The Other market includes alternative types of E-Gates that may use different technologies or offer additional features. Each type offers unique benefits and options for airports seeking to streamline their security and boarding processes.</p></p>
