Anode binders play a crucial role in ensuring the stability and performance of the battery, while cathode binders help improve the overall efficiency and lifespan of the battery. Both types of binders are vital in the production of high-quality lithium batteries, and they are widely used in various industries such as electronics, automotive, and energy storage.</p></p>
