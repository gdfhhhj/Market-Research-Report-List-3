Fly ash-based geopolymer cements are made from fly ash, a waste material from coal power plants. Ferro-sialate-based geopolymer cement incorporates ferro-sialate compounds in its production process. Each type of geopolymer cement offers unique benefits and applications, making them environmentally friendly alternatives to traditional Portland cement. These options cater to different industries and construction needs, providing sustainable and durable building solutions.</p></p>
