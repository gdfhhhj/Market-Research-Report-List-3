<p><strong>The Embedded Temperature Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Contact</li><li>Contact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The embedded temperature sensor market includes non-contact and contact types. Non-contact sensors measure temperature from a distance without physical contact, using technologies like infrared or optical sensors. This type is commonly used in industrial applications where accurate readings are crucial. Contact sensors require direct contact with the object to measure temperature, using methods like thermocouples or resistance temperature detectors. This type is often used in consumer electronics and home appliances for precise temperature monitoring. Both types cater to different industries and applications based on the specific requirements.</p></p>
