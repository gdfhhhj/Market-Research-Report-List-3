<p><p>Rock formation drilling rigs are categorized into three main types: rotary, impact, and others. Impact drilling rigs use a hammering action to break through harder rock formations, providing faster penetration rates. Other types of drilling rigs include diamond coring rigs and sonic drilling rigs, each with its own unique capabilities and applications in the rock drilling industry.</p></p>
