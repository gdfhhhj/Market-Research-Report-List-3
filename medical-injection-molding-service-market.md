<p><p>Medical injection molding service market types can be categorized into two major segments: thermoplastics and non-thermoplastics. Thermoplastics are materials that can be melted and re-melted multiple times without undergoing any significant change in their properties. These materials are widely used in medical devices and equipment due to their flexibility, easy processing, and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, non-thermoplastics are materials that cannot be melted and reused. These materials are less commonly used in medical injection molding but may be suitable for specific applications requiring unique properties or performance characteristics.</p></p>
