<p><p>EMI films are used to block electromagnetic interference in electronic devices. There are two main types of EMI film markets: metal-mesh film and coating film. Metal-mesh films have a layer of tiny metal wires embedded in the material to block electromagnetic signals. Coating films have a thin layer of conductive material applied to the surface of the device. Both types of EMI films help to improve the performance and reliability of electronic devices by reducing interference from external sources.</p></p>
