<p><strong>The Ventilated Curtain Wall Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unitised Type</li><li>Stick Built Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ventilated curtain walls are commonly classified into two main types: Unitised Type and Stick Built Type. The Unitised Type involves prefabricated panels that are assembled off-site and then installed on the building's façade as a single unit. This method allows for faster installation and improved quality control. The Stick Built Type, on the other hand, involves assembling the curtain wall on-site, piece by piece. While this method may be more time-consuming, it allows for greater customization and flexibility in design. Both types cater to different market needs and preferences.</p></p>
