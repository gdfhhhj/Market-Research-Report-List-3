<p><p>Mica band heaters are classified into two types based on their sheath material: stainless steel sheath and aluminized steel sheath. Stainless steel sheath band heaters are made of high-quality stainless steel, which provides excellent resistance to corrosion and high temperatures. These heaters are commonly used in applications where cleanliness and hygiene are crucial, such as food processing and medical equipment. Aluminized steel sheath band heaters, on the other hand, are made of steel coated with aluminum. They offer good resistance to high temperatures and low maintenance requirements, making them suitable for various industrial and commercial heating applications.</p></p>
