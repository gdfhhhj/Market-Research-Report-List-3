<p><p>Handheld dental scanners are commonly categorized based on the type of light source they use for scanning. Two main types are lasers light sources and LED light sources. Laser light sources produce a narrow and precise beam of light that can capture detailed images of the teeth and gums. On the other hand, LED light sources emit a broader and less focused light for scanning. Both types of scanners are used in dental procedures for obtaining accurate digital representations of the oral cavity.</p></p>
