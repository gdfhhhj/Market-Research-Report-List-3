<p><p>Digital Tunneling Magnetoresistance (TMR) sensors can be classified into three main types based on their operating principles: Omnipolar, Bipolar, and Unipolar. Omnipolar sensors can detect both North and South pole magnetic fields, making them versatile in various applications. Bipolar sensors are sensitive to both positive and negative magnetic fields, providing accurate measurements in both directions. Unipolar sensors only respond to one magnetic pole, simplifying their implementation in specific applications. Each type offers unique advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
