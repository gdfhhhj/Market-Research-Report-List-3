<p><p>Zirconia crowns are popular in the dental market and are available in two main types: layered zirconium crowns and monolithic zirconium crowns. Layered zirconium crowns are made of a zirconium core covered with layers of porcelain, providing a natural look and high strength. On the other hand, monolithic zirconium crowns are a single-piece structure of solid zirconia, offering excellent durability and aesthetic appeal. The choice between the two types depends on factors like cost, aesthetics, and patient preference.</p></p>
