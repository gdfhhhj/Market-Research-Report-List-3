<p><p>Airborne multispectral cameras come in two main types: single-lens multispectral cameras and multi-lens multispectral cameras. Single-lens cameras use a single lens to capture multiple spectral bands simultaneously, providing a cost-effective solution for capturing multispectral images. On the other hand, multi-lens cameras use multiple lenses to capture individual spectral bands separately, offering higher resolution and accuracy in capturing multispectral data. Both types of cameras play a crucial role in various industries such as agriculture, forestry, environmental monitoring, and resource management.</p></p>
