<p><ul><li>Passive Keyless Access (PKES)</li><li>Remote Keyless Access (RKES)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Passive Keyless Entry System (PKES) in the automotive industry refers to a technology where the vehicle automatically detects the key fob within close proximity, allowing the driver to unlock and start the car without pressing any buttons. On the other hand, Remote Keyless Entry System (RKES) requires the driver to press a button on the key fob to unlock or start the vehicle from a distance. Both systems provide convenience and enhanced security for drivers, with PKES being more seamless and user-friendly.</p></p>
