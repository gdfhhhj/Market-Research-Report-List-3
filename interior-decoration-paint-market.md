<p><ul><li>Waterborne</li><li>Solvent-Borne</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The interior decoration paint market can be divided into two main types: waterborne and solvent-borne. Waterborne paints are made with water as the main solvent, making them eco-friendly and easy to clean up. On the other hand, solvent-borne paints use chemical solvents like mineral spirits, making them more durable but less environmentally friendly. Both types have their own set of advantages and disadvantages, catering to different consumer preferences and needs in the interior decoration paint market.</p></p>
