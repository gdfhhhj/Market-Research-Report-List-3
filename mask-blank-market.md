<p><strong>The Mask Blank Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Reflectance Chrome-film Mask Blanks</li><li>Attenuated Phase Shift Mask Blanks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Low reflectance chrome-film mask blanks are designed to reduce unwanted reflection during the photolithography process, resulting in higher image contrast and improved resolution. They are commonly used in semiconductor manufacturing for advanced microchip production. Attenuated phase shift mask blanks, on the other hand, use a special material to control the phase of light passing through the mask, enabling precise control of light intensity and improving image resolution. Both types of mask blanks are critical components in the production of high-performance semiconductor devices.</p></p>
