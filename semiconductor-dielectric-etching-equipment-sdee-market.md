<p><p>Semiconductor Dielectric Etching Equipment (SDEE) market consists of two main types: Wet etching equipment and Dry etching equipment. Wet etching equipment utilizes liquid chemicals to remove material from semiconductor wafers, while Dry etching equipment uses plasma or gas phase etchants for the same purpose. Wet etching is preferred for its simplicity and cost-effectiveness, while Dry etching offers higher precision and control over the etching process. Both types of equipment play a crucial role in semiconductor manufacturing processes to achieve the desired patterns and structures on the wafers.</p></p>
