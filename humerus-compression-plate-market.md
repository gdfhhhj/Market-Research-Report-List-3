<p><p>Humerus Compression Plates are used in orthopedic surgery to stabilize fractures of the humerus bone. There are two main types of Humerus Compression Plates: Distal Plates and Proximal Plates. Distal Plates are placed towards the lower end of the humerus bone, while Proximal Plates are placed towards the upper end. Distal Plates are more commonly used for fractures near the elbow, while Proximal Plates are used for fractures near the shoulder. Both types of plates provide stability and support for the fractured bone during the healing process.</p></p>
