<p><p>Masking tapes market types include silicon-based adhesives, acrylic-based adhesives, rubber-based adhesives, and non-adhesive tapes. Silicon-based adhesives are known for their high temperature resistance and easy removal. Acrylic-based adhesives offer good adhesion to a variety of surfaces and are commonly used in automotive, painting, and construction industries. Rubber-based adhesives provide strong adhesion and are suitable for rough surfaces. Non-adhesive tapes are used for bundling, wrapping, and sealing applications where adhesive residue is not desirable.</p></p>
