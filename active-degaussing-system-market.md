<p><p>Active degaussing systems are used to neutralize the magnetic signature of ships, submarines, and other vessels to protect them from magnetic mines. There are two main types of active degaussing systems: External Degaussing Systems and Shipboard Degaussing Systems. External degaussing systems are installed on the exterior of the vessel and use coils to generate a magnetic field to counteract the ship's own magnetic field. Shipboard degaussing systems are installed within the vessel and utilize coils and sensors to continuously monitor and adjust the magnetic signature.</p></p>
