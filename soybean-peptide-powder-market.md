<p><p>There are three main types of Soybean Peptide Powder markets based on their origin and production process. Endogenous Peptide Powder is derived naturally from soybeans and contains peptides that are naturally present in the plant. Synthetic Peptide Powder is artificially created in a laboratory environment to mimic the peptides found in soybeans. Enzymatic Peptide Powder is produced through enzymatic hydrolysis of soy protein, resulting in the formation of bioactive peptides. Each type of Soybean Peptide Powder offers distinct benefits and applications in various industries.</p></p>
