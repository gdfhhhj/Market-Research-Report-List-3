<p><p>The medical diagnostic X-ray imaging device market can be classified into two types: stationary X-rays and portable X-rays. Stationary X-rays refer to imaging devices that are permanently installed in healthcare facilities, offering a fixed location for patients to undergo X-ray examinations. On the other hand, portable X-rays are mobile devices that can be easily transported to the patient's bedside or used in remote locations, allowing for on-the-spot diagnostic imaging. Both types serve the purpose of capturing internal images of patients to aid healthcare professionals in diagnosing and treating various medical conditions.</p></p>
