<p><p>The in-vehicle infotainment market can be categorized into three types: embedded, tethered, and integrated. Embedded infotainment systems are built into the vehicle's dashboard and are not dependent on external devices. Tethered infotainment systems, on the other hand, rely on external devices such as smartphones or tablets, which need to be connected to access the features. Lastly, integrated infotainment systems combine both embedded and tethered features, offering a seamless user experience with built-in as well as connected functionalities. These options cater to different user preferences and requirements in the automotive industry.</p></p>
