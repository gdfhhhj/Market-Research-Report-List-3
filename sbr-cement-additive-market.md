<p><p>SBR Cement Additive market comes in two main types - SBR Latex and SBR Powder. SBR Latex is a liquid form of the additive that is directly mixed with cement to enhance its properties such as bonding strength, flexibility, and water resistance. On the other hand, SBR Powder is a dry form of the additive that is mixed with water before combining it with cement. Both types of SBR additives are widely used in the construction industry for various applications.</p></p>
