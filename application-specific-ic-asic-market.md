<p><strong>The Application Specific IC ASIC Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full-Custom Design</li><li>Standard-Cell Based</li><li>Gate-Array Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Application Specific IC (ASIC) market consists of three main types based on design approach: Full-Custom Design, Standard-Cell Based, and Gate-Array Based. Full-Custom Design involves designing every component from scratch, providing high performance and low power consumption but requires more time and resources. Standard-Cell Based uses pre-designed logic cells for faster development with moderate customization options. Gate-Array Based offers high flexibility with prefabricated general-purpose logic gates that can be configured for specific applications, making it easier and quicker to design but sacrificing some performance and power efficiency.</p></p>
