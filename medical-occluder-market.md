<p><p>The medical occluder market consists of various types of occluders used for different medical conditions. LAA occluders are employed to seal Left Atrial Appendage, while PFO occluders are used to close Patent Foramen Ovale. These occluders help in minimizing the risk associated with these cardiac conditions and are an essential part of the medical device market.</p></p>
