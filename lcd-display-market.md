<p><p>LCD Displays are commonly divided into three types: Static, Simple Matrix, and Active Matrix. Static displays are simple, low-resolution screens with limited functionality. Simple Matrix displays offer slightly better performance with higher resolution and basic graphics capabilities. Active Matrix displays are the most advanced, providing high resolution, color accuracy, and fast response times. These displays are commonly used in smartphones, laptops, and TVs due to their superior image quality and versatility. Each type of display caters to different market needs and price points.</p></p>
