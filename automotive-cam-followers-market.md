<p><ul><li>Flat Cam Follower</li><li>Roller Cam Follower</li><li>Needle Cam Follower</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive cam followers are mechanical components used in engines to convert rotary motion into linear motion. Flat cam followers have a simple design and are commonly used in low-load applications. Roller cam followers have cylindrical rollers that provide higher load capacity and reduced friction. Needle cam followers have needle-shaped rollers that offer even greater load capacity and reduced friction compared to roller cam followers. Each type of cam follower is suited to different applications based on load requirements and operating conditions in the automotive industry.</p></p>
