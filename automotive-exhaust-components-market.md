<p><p>The automotive exhaust components market consists of various types of components such as manifold mufflers, clamps, stack pipes, manifold pipes, and connectors. Manifold mufflers are used to reduce noise from the exhaust system, while clamps are used to connect different components. Stack pipes are designed to release exhaust gases away from the vehicle. Manifold pipes channel exhaust gases from the engine to the exhaust system. Connectors are used to join different exhaust components together, ensuring efficient performance of the exhaust system.</p></p>
