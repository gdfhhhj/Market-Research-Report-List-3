<p><p>Ultra Low Refrigerators are available in two main market types: Upright Refrigerators and Chest Refrigerators. Upright Refrigerators are tall and narrow, with shelves that make it easy to organize and access contents. They are suitable for storing smaller items and are often used in laboratories and pharmacies. On the other hand, Chest Refrigerators have a horizontal orientation and a top-opening lid, providing a larger storage capacity for bulkier items. They are commonly used in medical facilities and research labs for storing vaccines, biological samples, and other temperature-sensitive materials.</p></p>
