<p><p>The Ice Melting Products market can be categorized into two main types: Liquid Ice Melting Products and Solid Ice Melting Products. Liquid ice melting products, such as liquid brine solutions or liquid calcium chloride, are applied directly to ice and snow to accelerate melting. Solid ice melting products, such as rock salt or calcium magnesium acetate, are granular materials that are spread on icy surfaces to lower the freezing point of water and melt the ice. Both types of products are commonly used for snow and ice removal in various settings.</p></p>
