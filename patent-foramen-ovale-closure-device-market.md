<p><p>A Patent Foramen Ovale (PFO) Closure Device is a medical device used to close a small hole in the heart called a patent foramen ovale. A PFO is a common heart defect present in about 25% of the population. It is typically harmless and goes undetected in most cases. However, in some individuals, a PFO can allow blood clots to travel from the right side of the heart to the left side, potentially causing strokes or other complications.</p><p>The PFO Closure Device is designed to be inserted through a catheter and positioned in the heart to close the hole. Once in place, the device provides a barrier between the two chambers of the heart, preventing blood clots from crossing over.</p><p>The global PFO Closure Device market is expected to experience steady growth in the coming years.
