<p><p>A Semiconductor Bonding Machine is used in the semiconductor industry for the packaging of semiconductor devices. There are two main types of bonding machines - Wire Bonder and Die Bonder. Wire bonders are used to connect the semiconductor device to the package using thin wires, while die bonders are used to attach the semiconductor die to the package. Both types of bonding machines play a crucial role in the manufacturing process of semiconductor devices, ensuring reliable and high-quality connections for optimal performance.</p></p>
