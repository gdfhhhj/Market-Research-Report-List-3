<p><p>Crew Management Systems (CMS) are software solutions used in the aviation industry to manage crew operations effectively. There are two types of CMS markets: On-Cloud and Server-Based. On-Cloud CMS refers to a system where the software and data are hosted on a cloud server, allowing users to access and manage crew information remotely through an internet connection. On the other hand, Server-Based CMS refers to a system where the software and data are stored on a local server within the organization, requiring users to access and manage the system within the organization's network.</p></p>
