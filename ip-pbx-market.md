<p><p>The IP PBX market consists of various types of products and services such as SIP Phones, VoIP Phones, IP PBX Servers, and VoIP Gateway. SIP Phones are devices that use Session Initiation Protocol to make calls over the internet. VoIP Phones are specifically designed to work with Voice over Internet Protocol technology. IP PBX Servers are the central hubs for managing VoIP calls within an organization. VoIP Gateways are devices that convert traditional phone calls into digital signals for transmission over IP networks.</p></p>
