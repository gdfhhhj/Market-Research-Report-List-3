<p><p>The Focused Ion Beam (FIB) market has various applications such as etching, imaging, deposition, and others. Etching refers to the process of removing material from a surface through the use of ions, enabling precise and controlled fabrication. Imaging involves creating high-resolution images of a sample by scanning it with ions, offering detailed analysis and examination. Deposition entails the deposition of materials onto a surface using ions, enabling the creation of thin films or coatings. Finally, the "others" category encompasses additional FIB applications like circuit editing, failure analysis, and nanofabrication.</p></p>
