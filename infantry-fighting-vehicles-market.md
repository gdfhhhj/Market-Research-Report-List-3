<p><p>Infantry Fighting Vehicles (IFVs) are military vehicles designed for transporting infantry and providing them with protection and firepower during combat. There are two main types of IFVs based on their mobility systems: crawler type and wheel type. Crawler-type IFVs employ a tracked system, consisting of continuous tracks that offer enhanced maneuverability on rough terrains, while wheel-type IFVs utilize wheels for higher speed and better performance on paved roads. Both types serve different operational purposes, with crawler-type IFVs being more suitable for off-road operations and wheel-type IFVs for urban or highway deployments.</p></p>
