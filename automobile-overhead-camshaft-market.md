<p><p>The Automobile Overhead Camshaft market consists of three main types, which include Single Overhead Camshaft (SOHC), Double Overhead Camshaft (DOHC), and Other market types. SOHC engines have one camshaft per cylinder head, operating both the intake and exhaust valves. DOHC engines have two camshafts per cylinder head, separate between the intake and exhaust valves. Other market types may include engines with variable valve timing, camless engines, or other variations in camshaft design to optimize performance and efficiency in automobiles.</p></p>
