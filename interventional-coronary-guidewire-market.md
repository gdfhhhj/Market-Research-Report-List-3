<p><strong>The Interventional Coronary Guidewire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Guidewires</li><li>Specialty Guidewires</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Interventional Coronary Guidewire market consists of two main types - Conventional Guidewires and Specialty Guidewires. Conventional guidewires are standard tools used for navigating through the coronary arteries during procedures like angioplasty. Specialty guidewires, on the other hand, are designed for specific applications such as chronic total occlusion (CTO) or complex lesion crossing. These wires have unique features that allow for better maneuverability and access to challenging coronary lesions, ultimately improving procedural outcomes.</p></p>
