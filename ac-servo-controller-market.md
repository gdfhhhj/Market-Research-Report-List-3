<p><p>AC Servo Controller market can be classified into two main types: Uniaxial and Multiaxial. Uniaxial controllers are designed to control the motion of a single axis in a machine while multiaxial controllers are capable of controlling multiple axes simultaneously. Uniaxial controllers are typically used in simpler machines where only one axis of motion is required, while multiaxial controllers are more suitable for complex machines that require precise control over multiple axes of movement. Both types cater to different industries and applications based on their specific requirements.</p></p>
