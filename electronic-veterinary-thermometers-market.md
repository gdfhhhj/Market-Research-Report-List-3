<p><p>Electronic veterinary thermometers are available in two main types: anal type and ear type. The anal type thermometers are inserted into the rectum of animals to measure their body temperature. They offer accurate readings and are commonly used in veterinary practices. On the other hand, ear type thermometers are designed to measure the temperature by placing them in the ear canal, making them less invasive for animals. They are quick and convenient to use, especially for animals that may be resistant to rectal temperature measurements.</p></p>
