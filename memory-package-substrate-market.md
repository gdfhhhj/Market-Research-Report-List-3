<p><ul><li>WB BGA</li><li>FC BGA</li><li>3D IC</li><li>WL CSP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Memory Package Substrate Market Types include WB BGA (Wire Bond BGA), FC BGA (Flip Chip BGA), 3D IC (Three-Dimensional Integrated Circuit), and WL CSP (Wafer Level Chip Scale Package). </p><p>WB BGA utilizes wire bonding for electrical connections, while FC BGA uses flip chip technology for superior performance. 3D IC packaging involves stacking multiple layers of integrated circuits for higher density and performance. WL CSP is a packaging technology where the chip is directly packaged on the wafer level for smaller form factor and higher reliability.</p></p>
